    Memcache is in-memory only. Redis periodically & asynchronously writes out state to disk, so when you restart your server, you dont lose all your sessions/cache.

    Also, since the Redis protocol is a superset of the Memcached protocol, you can use it as a backend to anything that understands memcached.
